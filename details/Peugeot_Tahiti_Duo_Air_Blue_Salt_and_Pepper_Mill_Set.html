Elevate Your Table with This Premium Salt and Pepper Mill Set

Enhance every dish with the perfect balance of flavor and aroma. This Peugeot Tahiti Duo Air Blue Salt and Pepper Mill Set is designed to bring both elegance and precision to your kitchen. The dual-tone design makes it easy to distinguish between salt and pepper at a glance, ensuring you always reach for the right one.

Crafted in France from sustainably sourced PEFC-certified beech wood, these mills are built for durability and environmental responsibility. The 2-stage grinding mechanism features case-hardened steel teeth that stay razor-sharp and corrosion-resistant, delivering a perfectly fine or coarse grind with each turn.

By incorporating a crack-first grinding process, these mills are engineered to release the full flavor and aroma of your ingredients, making your meals more enticing. The adjustable grind settings allow for versatility, making this set ideal for both professional chefs and everyday home cooks.

Whether you're seasoning a steak, enhancing a soup, or garnishing a fresh salad, these mills add a touch of sophistication and convenience. Place them on any kitchen countertop and enjoy their stylish presence while creating culinary excellence. A must-have for any kitchen enthusiast looking to refine their cooking experience.

Key Features:

  • Dual-tone design for easy identification of salt and pepper
  • Made in France using PEFC-certified beech wood
  • 2-stage grinding mechanism with sharp, long-lasting steel teeth
  • Designed for maximum flavor release with a crack-first grinding process
  • Adjustable grind settings to suit your cooking preferences
