Famous Dino Under Sink Mat

The Famous Dino Under Sink Mat is a high-quality silicone drip tray that helps keep your kitchen and bathroom organized and free from water damage. Designed for versatility and durability, it adapts perfectly to different cabinet sizes and shapes, ensuring a secure fit without the need for permanent installation. This mat is not only functional but also safe for use around pets, and it can double as a feeding mat or a disinfecting surface for shoes.

Key Features

  • Constructed from premium, BPA-free silicone for safe and durable use
  • Adjustable dimensions to fit most cabinets up to 28" x 19"
  • Easy to cut and fold for custom fit around pipes and fixtures
  • Built-in raised edges that contain spills and prevent leaks
  • Designed to hold up to 1.5 gallons of water for added protection

Why Choose This Mat?

This under sink mat is perfect for keeping your workspace clean and dry, whether you're organizing your kitchen sink or protecting your bathroom vanity. The waterproof construction ensures that no spills or stains get onto your cabinets, while the flexible design makes it easy to fit into tight spaces. It's an excellent solution for pet owners or anyone looking for a reliable utility mat that can be used in multiple rooms around the home.

Ideal For:

  • Under-sink storage and organization
  • Protecting cabinet surfaces from water damage
  • Creating a clean and safe environment for pets
  • Disinfecting shoes or serving as a feeding station
